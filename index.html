So lets say you have a website that looks like this

A simple input box asking for the user's name. What couldn't go wrong?

What's wrong with my website!?

If you have a very basic website, nothing is wrong with that input box. Let's expand our website a little, and link it to a database to see the catastrophic consequences of one simple input box.

When your input box takes the exact message it sees and sends it to your database, it encourages...
ATTACKERS! to inject scripts into your database!
Hang on hang on... what do you mean "Inject scripts"?
"Injecting scripts" essentially means instructing your website to do things you didn't design it for.
Imagine you have a website designed exclusively to showcase pictures of dogs. As an attacker, I might inject a script to turn them all into cats!
